Welcome to Celby!!

What does Celby do?

Celby lets you search for celebrations all over the world in any given city or on any given day, so that you always have a reason to celebrate! Celby's aim is to bring joy (and cake) on any given day, or suprise you with a celebration that you had no idea about.

Inspiration:

The news is often so boring and sad that it could be renamed "The depressing facts." Taking inspiration from "The Happy Broadcast" - which sends out happy news every day.
